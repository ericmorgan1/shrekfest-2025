★ ABOUT SHREKFEST ★

WELCOME to the world's premier celebration of love, life and Shrek! Join us for one day of ogre-sized fun in the swamp! Shrekfest began as a small gathering of fans and has grown into a huge phenomenon.

Featuring costume contests, onion eating competitions, a screening of the movie, and so much more!
